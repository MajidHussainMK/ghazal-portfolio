About Me

With years of experience in physiotherapy and academic excellence, I am committed to helping patients achieve optimal health and training the next generation of healthcare professionals.

As a dedicated physiotherapist, I specialize in evidence-based treatment approaches that address musculoskeletal conditions, rehabilitation, and preventive care. My practice combines clinical expertise with compassionate patient care.

In my role as a university lecturer, I am passionate about advancing physiotherapy education, conducting research, and mentoring students to become skilled and empathetic practitioners.
